Our Work






Clinics


Selcouth clinics are spread out around the globe and is where you as a client or user, will travel to in order to be inseminated with the crafted embryo. These Selcouth clinics were first established in 2015 and as we continue to grow, more clinics are being built to make this process more accessible for people from all walks of life and geographical locations. Our staff in the Selcouth clinics are highly trained and work tirelessly to ensure that you as a client are treated with the highest level of respect and served with the highest level of patient care. Our insemination specialists are vetted to ensure that they reach our level of professionalism and are qualified to a high degree to be responsible for your insemination process. Our clinics are equiped to deal with any emergencies should one occur, they're also stocked with top of the line insemination equipment, comfortable beds and rooms to sleep if you decide to stay the night after your insemination. At Selcouth we want you to feel safe and comfortable with us and with your journey.
